About

Kristofer Dowdell is a hobbyist photographer and amateuer web designer based out of Washington, D.C. He has a strong passion for visual communication and has also dabbled in videography and graphic design. Kris draws most of his inspiration from architecture, street, and minimalist photography, as those subjects and style tend to be his primary focus. An avid gamer, he spends most of his free time equally divided between gaming, learning and improving his web design skills, and planning his next international destination. Kris has an extensive background in television news production and holds a bachelor's degree in mass communication from Frostburg State University, where he concentrated in audio/video production.
